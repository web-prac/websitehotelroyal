Hotel Royal is one of the most recognized names in the grand destination circuit of Chandigarh.

Hotel Royal is currently one of the top ranking premier business hotels in the city of Chandigarh and it was first established 30 years back and the main aim of the hotel was to provide the valued guests with unparalleled comfort and excellent standards of hospitability and unforgettable experience, which made it possible to be a premier business hotel. And for the last three decades, Hotel Royal has been able to create a successful panorama of the new business esthetics and a host to various business executives as well.

Hotel Royal is comprised of 51 air-conditioned deluxe and executive rooms which are newly renovated and come with state of art amenities and luxurious interiors and they are also spacious as well.

There are also 6 suites which are elegantly designed with ranges of hues of wood, along with the wooden flooring and the room furnished with soft cream and beige, creating an elegant and sophisticated interior. There is also a presidential suite that consists of two plush bedrooms, along with a living room that represents a unique style of the colonial era by the international designers. In short, it creates a classical statement of grandeur and elegance.
